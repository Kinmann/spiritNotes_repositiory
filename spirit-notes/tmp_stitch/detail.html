The Highland
Vanguard 1974

Peat Oak Fruit Spice Smoke Honey

Profile Overview

A masterclass in restraint and aging. This expression represents the pinnacle of the mid-70s distillation era, offering a profile that is as complex as it is smooth.

ABV 54.2%
Region Highlands

The initial nose is an evocative journey through a rain-drenched pine forest, followed closely by the warmth of toasted hazelnuts and a whisper of wildflower honey. There is a weight to this spirit that commands attention before it even touches the palate.

Palate & Evolution

Upon entry, the texture is remarkably oily and rich. Decades in the cask have transformed the aggressive highland malt into a silky, multifaceted experience. Mid-palate, you'll find deep layers of dried apricots, dark chocolate, and a sophisticated medicinal smoke that lingers without overwhelming. It’s a delicate balance that few distilleries have ever mastered.

"A liquid time capsule that captures the soul of the 1970s Highlands distillation style."

The Finish

Long, warming, and exceptionally clean. The oak presence is significant but refined, leaving a final impression of leather, pipe tobacco, and a faint hint of sea salt. This is not just a drink; it is a meditation on time and craft.
