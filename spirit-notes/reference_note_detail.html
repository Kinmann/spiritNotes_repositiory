Tasting Experience

Deep mahogany color, complex aroma of smoke and sea spray. A classic Islay punch that evolves into salted caramel.

The entry is oily and viscous, immediately coating the tongue in a layer of medicinal peat and char. Middle notes bring a surprising sweetness—think grilled peaches and brown sugar—before the long, lingering finish of iodine and white pepper takes over.
